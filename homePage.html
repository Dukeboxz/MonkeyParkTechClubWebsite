Welcome to the webpage of Monkey Park Tech Club. A club set up to help 7 - 12 year olds discover the joy of coding and electronics and then help them develop their skills in a fun and safe environment. The club is run by volunteers who all have experience of working and coding in the tech sector. Each month the volunteers aim to give a range of activites for kids no matter what their ability level.
